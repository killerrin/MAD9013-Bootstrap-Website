Ten Steps for United Enviromental Health

  1. Switch off anything that uses electricity when not in use
  2. Unplug devices when possible
  3. Conserve Water.. and bring your own Reusable Water Bottle
  4. Instead of plastic bags, bring your own reusable shopping bag
  5. Reduce, Reuse Recycle
  6. Stop littering!
  7. Work from home
  8. Bike and Walk as much as possible, and when not possible, Carpool or use Public Transit
  9. Plant some trees or start a garden
  10. Reduce usage of pesticides
